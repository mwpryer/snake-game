Information

Control the snake using WASD or ↑←↓→ to move it up, left, down, or right respectively. On mobile devices simply swipe in the direction you wish the snake to travel.

Moving the snake over pieces of food increases its length and your game score. Any collisions of the snake with itself or the surrounding boundaries results in gameover.

Some settings can be changed in the options panel. A game can be paused at any time by clicking outside the game widget.

Have fun!
